Banana Muffin Recipe

Description:

Banana muffin cookie recipe by ABI_GODFREY with a few tweaks! This recipe creates delicious gluten-free banana muffins

Ingredients:

  • 1â€‰3/4 cups oat flour*
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2teaspoon salt
  • 3 large bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
*to create oat flour you can simple blend old-fashioned rolled oats or quick-cooking oats in a blender or food processor.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat muffin pans with non-stick spray, or use paper liners. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
  2. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a large bowl. Fold in flour mixture, and mix until smooth. Scoop into muffin pans.
  3. Bake in preheated oven. Bake mini muffins for 10 to 15 minutes, and large muffins for 25 to 30 minutes. Muffins will spring back when lightly tapped.
